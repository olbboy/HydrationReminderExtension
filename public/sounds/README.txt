Add the following MP3 sound files to this directory:

1. water-drop.mp3 - A simple water drop sound
2. bell.mp3 - A bell chime sound
3. chime.mp3 - A pleasant notification chime
4. birds.mp3 - Bird chirping sounds
5. stream.mp3 - Water stream or river sounds

These files will be used for notification sounds in the Hydration Reminder extension.
Files should be short (1-3 seconds) and in MP3 format for best compatibility.

You can find free sound effects from various websites like:
- Freesound.org
- ZapSplat.com
- SoundBible.com
- Pixabay.com (Sound section)

Make sure to check the licensing terms of any sounds you download to ensure they can be used in your extension.
